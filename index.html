El Pollo Loco

PEPE PELIGROSO

"Collect the coins and make Pepe rich. Be careful, the small and big chickens are against you and will damage you. Jump on them to defeat the chickens. Don't forget the hot sauces, you will need them later for the final boss. Throw the hot sauces (hot bottles) at the final boss and you will win the game and made Pepe rich."

LEFT = Move Left
RIGHT = Move Right
SPACE = Jump
D = Throw Bottles
